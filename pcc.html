Primary Class Curriculum

At the Primary Wing, we believe that the mind, like a parachute, works best when opened. Our curriculum and evaluation procedures are designed to maximize studentsâ€™ knowledge, potential, and talents. Our classrooms are colorful, joyful, well-ventilated, and provide a friendly environment with a theme-based curriculum. In addition to the diverse academic and co-curricular activities, our curriculum emphasizes the importance of holistic development. By integrating various clubs, creative arts, sports, and cultural events, we ensure that students not only excel academically but also develop essential life skills.


















1) Core Subjects: The curriculum includes English, Hindi, Math, Environmental Studies, Moral Values, Art and Craft, General Knowledge, and Computer Literacy Programme.
2) Morning Assemblies: Daily assemblies to foster a sense of community and routine.
3) Clubs and Labs: English Club, Math Club, and Computer Laboratories to enhance subject-specific skills.
4) Creative Activities: Handicraft, Puppet Making, Painting, Sketching, and Paper Folding to encourage creativity.


















5) Cultural Arts: Rangoli & Floral Designing to explore cultural expressions.
6) Sports: Both indoor and outdoor games to promote physical fitness.
7) Storytelling and Debates: Storytelling sessions and Debate Club to develop communication skills.
8) Events and Competitions: Annual Concert, Social Science and Science Exhibitions, Child Fair with Parents, Sports Day, Olympiads, Special Assemblies, and various competitions to showcase talents and creativity.
