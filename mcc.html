Middle Class Curriculum

The curriculum for middle classes is designed to challenge students to ask questions, enhance skills, conduct in-depth research, analyze findings, and build conceptual understanding. Each child is supported to fulfill their unique potential in a balanced, student-friendly environment.In addition to fostering academic and co-curricular excellence, the Middle Classes Curriculum emphasizes the development of critical thinking and problem-solving skills. By engaging students in diverse activities and practical learning experiences, we aim to nurture well-rounded individuals who are prepared to navigate the complexities of the modern world with confidence and creativity.


















1) Academic Excellence: The middle wing maintains high standards in academics and co-curricular activities to prepare students for today’s competitive world.
2) CBSE Guidelines: The curriculum follows the guidelines issued by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE).
3) CCE Pattern: Continuous and Comprehensive Evaluation (CCE) is used, covering both scholastic and co-scholastic areas.
4) Scholastic Subjects: Includes English, Hindi, Mathematics, Science, Social Science, Artificial Intelligence, Moral Values, General Knowledge, and Art & Craft, with additional supplementary readers to foster reading habits.


















5) Co-Scholastic Activities: Focuses on life skills, environmental awareness, value education, performing arts, visual arts, and various competitions such as drawing, coloring, sketching, painting, sculpture, and craft work.
6) Health & Physical Education: Includes physical exercises, athletics, yoga, meditation, and sports like cricket, football, kho-kho, and indoor games.
7) Additional Activities: Includes class debates, essay writing, and inter-school competitions to enhance students’ skills and confidence.
8) Excursions and Educational Tours: Regular local and outstation excursions and educational tours provide practical learning experiences.
